So... Where should I start?

Education

I grew up in a country and time at which computers were not allowed to enter the school building.

The world by then had changed significantly because of technology and I wanted to join that wave. To add to that I was at boarding school for a few years so 5 our of 7 days I didn't have any access to what I was invested in.

I even had to stop my first business to make room for boarding school.

After many years of giving it a try and making the best of it I left the traditional education system. I wasn't performing to the best of my ability nor was I learning skills that would benefit me in my future life. At age 15 it was me, my parents and a person named Erik who helped me occasionally with self study. I was an entrepreneurial kid, I ran little businesses did my own things, jumped on new and exciting tech. Going solo made sense. I finally got the flexibility I needed to do what I wanted + I was studying for exams and achieved pretty good grades even.

At 15 I even attempted to study for my CCNA certification but I stopped as it felt dated and just made me hate what I liked.

So I'm a self thought person, I love reading up on new things, researching, trying stuff until it works and that's how I got my diploma.
